Our true love story

We met at school camp. I stole his cool hat and he gave me piggy back rides through the bush tracks. We held hands at the bus stop and fought for first place in PE in year 12 (I won). Our first kiss was in a hammock at the Hippy Shack- a regular weekend hangout.

We finished school and ventured overseas together, backpacking across Europe and making lifetime memories, realizing that this was the real deal… 8 years later, Dave (and Ellie) got down on one knee and popped the question, I finally answered "yes" after asking "have you asked Dad?!" (he later told me that he had taken 18 months to find the right moment to ask Dad, keeping the ring in the car boot!). There was champagne, family and friends- it was perfect! We packed our bags and made a sea change to the big smoke, starting a new adventure together.

I can't wait to marry my best friend, and I know he can't wait either!
